Our Creative Journey

From vision to installation
01

Vision & Design

We begin with your inspiration. Share your ideas, images, and garden dimensions. Our designers create detailed sketches and 3D models for your approval.

02

Material Selection

Choose from our premium materials based on your aesthetic preferences, climate conditions, and budget. We guide you through the unique qualities of each option.

03

Artisan Creation

Master sculptors transform raw materials into art. This stage takes 4-8 weeks depending on complexity, with progress photos shared along the way.

04

Global Delivery

Your sculpture is carefully packaged and shipped. Our team can arrange professional installation to ensure perfect placement in your garden sanctuary.
