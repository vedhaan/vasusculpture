Our Heritage

Where centuries-old craftsmanship meets contemporary design

Elevating Gardens Since 1998

Founded in the artistic heart of Tuscany, LuxStone began as a passion project of master sculptor Giovanni Rossi. Today, we continue his legacy by blending traditional stone carving techniques with modern design sensibilities.

Each sculpture is conceived and executed by artisans who have dedicated their lives to mastering stone. We believe in creating pieces that don't just adorn a garden, but become an integral part of its story and soul.

Our commitment to sustainable practices and ethical sourcing ensures that every creation not only enhances your space but also respects our planet for generations to come.

Our Creative Journey

From vision to installation

Vision & Design

We begin with your inspiration. Share your ideas, images, and garden dimensions. Our designers create detailed sketches and 3D models for your approval.

Material Selection

Choose from our premium materials based on your aesthetic preferences, climate conditions, and budget. We guide you through the unique qualities of each option.

Artisan Creation

Master sculptors transform raw materials into art. This stage takes 4-8 weeks depending on complexity, with progress photos shared along the way.

Global Delivery

Your sculpture is carefully packaged and shipped. Our team can arrange professional installation to ensure perfect placement in your garden sanctuary.
